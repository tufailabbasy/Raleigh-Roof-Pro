### Long Description (Full)
Raleigh Roof Pro is a premier roofing contractor based in Raleigh, NC, dedicated to protecting homes and businesses throughout the Triangle region. Founded in 2020, we have quickly established a reputation for reliability, craftsmanship, and superior customer service. 

We are proud to be a GAF Master Elite® certified contractor, a distinction held by only 2% of roofers nationwide, allowing us to offer industry-leading warranty protection. Our comprehensive services include residential shingle replacement, commercial flat roofing (TPO/EPDM/Metal), thorough roof inspections, and rapid storm damage restoration including insurance claim assistance.

Beyond roofing, we provide complete exterior solutions including seamless gutter installation and siding replacement. Whether you need a minor repair or a complete roof replacement, Raleigh Roof Pro delivers quality results backed by 5+ years of local experience and hundreds of five-star reviews.
