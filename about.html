Our Story

Since 2020, Raleigh Roof Pro has been protecting homes and businesses throughout the Raleigh area with quality roofing services you can trust. What started as a small, family-owned business has grown into one of the Triangle's most respected roofing contractors.

We've built our reputation one roof at a time, focusing on honest communication, superior craftsmanship, and exceptional customer service. Today, with over 500 roofs installed and countless satisfied customers, we remain committed to the same values that got us started: integrity, quality, and putting our customers first.

As a locally owned and operated company, we understand the unique challenges that Raleigh's climate presents to roofing systems. From intense summer heat to winter storms and everything in between, we know what it takes to keep your home protected year-round.
