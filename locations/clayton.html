Roofing Services in Clayton NC

Residential Roof Replacement

When your Clayton home needs a new roof, our team delivers seamless installation from start to finish. We help you select the right roofing materials for your home's style and your budget, provide detailed estimates with no hidden costs, and complete installations efficiently while minimizing disruption to your family. Our GAF Master Elite certification means access to the best warranty coverage in the industry.

Roof Repair Services

Not every roofing issue requires full replacement. Our repair services address leaks and water intrusion, storm damage including missing or damaged shingles, flashing problems around chimneys and vents, and general wear from aging. We provide honest assessments about whether repair or replacement makes more sense for your situation.

Storm Damage Restoration

Johnston County sees its share of severe weather, from summer thunderstorms to the occasional tropical system. When storms damage your Clayton home, we provide rapid response for emergency tarping, thorough damage assessment and documentation, insurance claim assistance, and quality permanent repairs.

Gutter and Siding Services

Complete exterior protection requires more than just roofing. We install and repair gutters to channel water safely away from your foundation, and we offer siding installation and repair to protect your home's walls and enhance curb appeal.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new roof cost in Clayton?

Roof replacement costs depend on roof size, pitch, material choice, and any necessary repairs to underlying decking. Most Clayton homes range from $8,000-$20,000 for quality asphalt shingle installation. We provide free estimates with detailed breakdowns.

Do you offer financing for Clayton homeowners?

Yes, we offer flexible financing options to help Clayton residents afford quality roofing. Various terms are available with competitive rates. Ask about current promotions during your estimate.

How quickly can you complete a roof replacement?

Most residential roof replacements in Clayton are completed in 1-3 days depending on roof size and complexity. Weather delays can extend timelines, but we communicate proactively about scheduling.

Are you licensed and insured in Johnston County?

Yes, Raleigh Roof Pro is fully licensed and insured to perform roofing work throughout North Carolina, including Clayton and all of Johnston County. We carry comprehensive liability and workers' compensation coverage.
