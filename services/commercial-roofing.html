commercial roofing Systems We Install

The success of any commercial roofing project depends heavily on selecting the right roofing system for your building's specific requirements. Raleigh Roof Pro is certified to install multiple commercial roofing systems, giving you access to the full range of options available in today's market. Our most popular commercial roofing options include TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin), EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer), modified bitumen, built-up roofing, metal roofing systems, and spray polyurethane foam.

TPO roofing has become increasingly popular among Triangle businesses due to its excellent energy efficiency, durability, and cost-effectiveness. This single-ply membrane roofing system reflects solar heat, reducing cooling costs during North Carolina's hot summers. commercial roofing FAQ

Answers to common questions about commercial roofing in Raleigh

How long does a commercial roof last?

commercial roofing lifespans vary by material. TPO and EPDM systems typically last 20-30 years with proper maintenance. Metal roofing can last 40-50 years or more. Built-up roofing systems generally last 15-20 years. Regular maintenance and prompt repairs can significantly extend the life of any commercial roofing system.

Can you work around our business operations?

Absolutely. We understand that business continuity is critical. Our team can work during off-hours, weekends, or in phases to minimize disruption to your operations. We'll develop a project schedule that accommodates your specific needs while maintaining efficient project completion.

What is the best commercial roofing material?

The best material depends on your building's specific requirements, budget, and long-term goals. TPO offers excellent energy efficiency and value. EPDM provides proven durability at lower cost. Metal systems offer maximum longevity. We'll assess your needs and recommend the optimal solution during your free consultation.

Do you offer commercial roof maintenance programs?

Yes, we offer comprehensive preventive maintenance programs designed to extend roof life and prevent costly emergency repairs. Our maintenance plans include semi-annual inspections, drainage system cleaning, flashing and sealant inspection, and minor repairs. Regular maintenance can extend your roof's lifespan by 25% or more.

Are you licensed and insured for commercial work?

Yes, Raleigh Roof Pro holds all required North Carolina licenses for commercial roofing work. We maintain comprehensive general liability insurance, workers' compensation coverage, and umbrella policies that meet or exceed the requirements of most commercial projects. Certificates of insurance are available upon request.
