Professional Gutter Services for Raleigh Homes

Your gutters are your home's first line of defense against water damage. Properly functioning gutters channel rainwater away from your foundation, preventing costly problems like basement flooding, soil erosion, and structural damage. At Raleigh Roof Pro, we provide comprehensive gutter installation, repair, and maintenance services throughout the Raleigh area.

North Carolina's climate brings significant rainfall, especially during spring and summer months. Without adequate gutter protection, water can pool around your foundation, seep into basements, and damage landscaping. Our team specializes in designing gutter systems specifically suited to handle the heavy downpours common in the Raleigh and Triangle region.

Whether you need new seamless gutter installation, gutter repairs, or protective gutter guards, our experienced technicians deliver quality workmanship backed by warranty protection. We work with premium materials from trusted manufacturers to ensure your gutter system performs reliably for decades.

Our Gutter Services

Seamless Gutter Installation

Seamless gutters are custom-fabricated on-site to fit your home's exact specifications. Unlike sectional gutters with joints every 10-20 feet, seamless gutters have no seams along the length of your roofline, which means fewer potential leak points and a cleaner appearance.

Our seamless gutter installation process includes precise measurement of your roofline, on-site fabrication using professional gutter machines, and expert installation with properly spaced hangers for maximum durability. We offer seamless gutters in 5-inch and 6-inch profiles to accommodate different roof sizes and rainfall volumes.

Gutter Replacement

If your existing gutters are sagging, pulling away from the fascia, showing visible rust or corrosion, or leaking at multiple points, replacement may be more cost-effective than repeated repairs. Our gutter replacement service includes complete removal of your old system, inspection of underlying fascia boards, and installation of a new gutter system designed for optimal performance.

Gutter Repair

Not all gutter problems require full replacement. Our repair services address common issues including leaking joints and seams, sagging or improperly pitched sections, loose or missing hangers, downspout disconnections, and minor hole patching. We assess the overall condition of your system and recommend repair versus replacement based on what makes the most sense for your situation.

Gutter Guard Installation

Tired of cleaning leaves and debris from your gutters every season? Gutter guards provide a protective cover that allows water to flow through while blocking leaves, pine needles, and other debris. We install several types of gutter protection systems including mesh screens, micro-mesh filters, and solid covers with surface tension technology.

Gutter guards significantly reduce maintenance requirements and prevent clogs that can cause water overflow and ice dams. While no system is 100% maintenance-free, quality gutter guards can reduce cleaning frequency from multiple times per year to occasional inspections.

Downspout Installation & Extensions

Downspouts carry water from your gutters down to ground level, but proper water management doesn't stop there. We install downspout extensions, underground drainage connections, and splash blocks to direct water safely away from your foundation. Proper downspout placement and extensions are critical for preventing foundation problems.

Gutter Materials We Offer

Aluminum Gutters

Aluminum is the most popular gutter material for good reason. It's lightweight, rust-resistant, affordable, and available in a wide range of colors to match your home's exterior. Aluminum gutters can be painted if you want to change colors later and typically last 20-30 years with proper maintenance.

Copper Gutters

For homeowners seeking a premium, distinctive look, copper gutters offer unmatched beauty and longevity. Copper develops an attractive patina over time and can last 50+ years. While the upfront cost is higher than aluminum, copper gutters add significant curb appeal and value to high-end homes.

Galvanized Steel Gutters

Steel gutters offer excellent strength and durability, making them suitable for areas with heavy snow loads or large debris. Galvanized coatings protect against rust, though steel gutters require more maintenance than aluminum to maximize their lifespan.

Signs You Need New Gutters

  • Visible cracks or splits: Even small cracks will expand over time and allow water to escape
  • Peeling paint or rust: Indicates moisture is not being properly channeled away
  • Sagging or pulling away: Gutters should remain level and securely attached
  • Water pooling around foundation: Suggests overflow or improper drainage
  • Mildew or water damage on siding: Water is escaping and damaging exterior walls
  • Basement water intrusion: Often caused by failed gutter systems
  • Eroded landscaping: Water overflow is washing away mulch and soil

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do new gutters cost in Raleigh?

Gutter costs vary based on linear footage, material choice, and home height. Aluminum seamless gutters typically range from $5-15 per linear foot installed. A typical single-story home with 150-200 linear feet of gutters might cost $1,000-$2,500 for aluminum. Contact us for a free estimate based on your specific home.

How long do gutters last?

With proper maintenance, aluminum gutters last 20-30 years, galvanized steel 15-25 years, and copper 50+ years. Regular cleaning and prompt repairs extend lifespan significantly.

Should gutters be replaced with the roof?

Not necessarily, but it's often convenient and cost-effective to do both at once. If your gutters are more than 15-20 years old, replacing them during a roof project saves on labor and ensures a perfectly integrated system.

What size gutters do I need?

Most homes use 5-inch gutters, but larger roofs or areas with heavy rainfall may benefit from 6-inch gutters. We assess your roof area and local rainfall to recommend the right size.

Are gutter guards worth it?

For most homeowners, gutter guards are a worthwhile investment. They reduce cleaning frequency, prevent clogs, and extend gutter lifespan. They're especially valuable if you have large trees near your home.
