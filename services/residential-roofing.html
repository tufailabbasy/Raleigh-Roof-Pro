Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about residential roofing in Raleigh

How long does a residential roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements in Raleigh are completed within 1-3 days, depending on the size of your home, complexity of the roof design, and weather conditions. Our experienced crews work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily routine while maintaining the highest quality standards. We'll provide you with a specific timeline during our initial consultation based on your project's unique requirements.

What is the average cost of a new roof in Raleigh, NC?

The cost of a new roof in Raleigh varies based on several factors including roof size, pitch complexity, material selection, and any necessary repairs to the underlying structure. A typical residential roof replacement ranges from $8,000 to $25,000 or more. We provide free, detailed estimates so you know exactly what to expect. Financing options are available to help make your investment more manageable.

How do I know if I need a roof replacement or just repairs?

Signs that indicate you may need a full replacement include widespread shingle damage, multiple leaks, sagging sections, or a roof that's more than 20 years old. However, localized damage from storms or isolated leaks can often be repaired cost-effectively. Our free inspection provides an honest assessment of your roof's condition with clear recommendations. We never recommend replacement when repairs will suffice.

Do you offer warranties on residential roofing work?

Yes, absolutely. As a GAF Master Elite® contractor, we offer the industry's strongest warranties including GAF's Golden Pledge Limited Warranty, which covers materials and workmanship for up to 25 years with no cost coverage. Additionally, we stand behind our workmanship with our own comprehensive guarantee. We're so confident in our work that we remain available to address any concerns long after your project is complete.

Can you work with my insurance company for storm damage claims?

Yes, we have extensive experience working with all major insurance companies on storm damage claims in the Raleigh area. We handle the entire claims process from initial documentation to adjuster meetings, ensuring you receive fair compensation for covered damage. Our thorough documentation and professional approach help streamline the claims process, making a stressful situation much easier for you.

What roofing materials are best for North Carolina's climate?

Architectural asphalt shingles are the most popular choice for Raleigh homes due to their excellent balance of durability, aesthetics, and value. Look for shingles rated for high wind resistance (130+ mph) and impact resistance (Class 4) to handle our occasional severe storms. Metal roofing is also an excellent option for homeowners seeking maximum longevity and energy efficiency. During your consultation, we'll recommend the best options based on your specific needs and preferences.
