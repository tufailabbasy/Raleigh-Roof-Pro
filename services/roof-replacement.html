Signs You Need a Roof Replacement

Knowing when to replace your roof versus when to repair it can save you thousands of dollars and prevent more serious damage to your home. While some roofing issues can be addressed with targeted repairs, there are certain signs that indicate a full replacement is the most cost-effective long-term solution. These signs include widespread shingle deterioration such as curling, cracking, or missing shingles across large areas of your roof. If your roof is approaching or has exceeded its expected lifespan—typically 20-25 years for asphalt shingles—replacement becomes increasingly necessary.

Other indicators that suggest it's time for a new roof include persistent leaks despite previous repair attempts, sagging or structural concerns visible from inside your attic, significant granule loss in your gutters and downspouts, and daylight visible through roof boards in your attic space. If you've noticed any of these warning signs, we encourage you to schedule a free inspection with our team. We provide honest assessments and will only recommend replacement when it's truly necessary for your situation.

Our Roof Replacement Process

At Raleigh Roof Pro, we've refined our roof replacement process over the years to deliver exceptional results efficiently and with minimal disruption to your daily life. Every project begins with a comprehensive inspection where we assess your existing roof condition, identify any underlying structural issues, take precise measurements, and discuss your material preferences and budget considerations. We then provide a detailed written estimate outlining all costs, materials, and timeline expectations.

Once you've approved the project, we coordinate a convenient installation date and handle all necessary permits. On installation day, our crews arrive promptly and begin by protecting your landscaping, siding, and outdoor furniture with tarps and boards. We carefully remove your existing roofing materials down to the deck, inspect the wood decking for damage (replacing any compromised sections as needed), and install new underlayment, ice and water shield, drip edge, and other protective components before installing your new roofing materials according to manufacturer specifications.

Throughout the installation process, we maintain a clean work site with ongoing debris removal. At the end of each day—and especially upon project completion—we conduct thorough cleanup including magnetic sweeping to collect any stray nails. Your final walkthrough with our project manager ensures complete satisfaction before we consider the job finished. We then register your warranties and provide all documentation for your records.

Roof Replacement FAQ

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements in Raleigh are completed in 1-3 days depending on roof size, complexity, and weather conditions. A typical 2,000 square foot home with a straightforward roof design can often be completed in a single day. More complex roofs with multiple levels, dormers, or steep pitches may require 2-3 days. We'll provide an accurate timeline estimate during your consultation.

How much does a new roof cost in Raleigh?

Roof replacement costs in Raleigh typically range from $8,000 to $25,000 or more depending on roof size, material selection, complexity, and any necessary repairs to the underlying structure. Architectural shingles generally cost less than designer shingles or metal roofing. We provide free, detailed estimates so you know exactly what to expect. Financing options are available to make your investment more manageable.

What warranties do you offer?

As a GAF Master Elite® contractor, we can offer GAF's strongest warranty options including the Golden Pledge Limited Warranty, which covers both materials and workmanship for up to 25 years. This means if there's ever an issue with your roof—whether related to materials or installation—you're covered at no additional cost. We also stand behind our work with our own comprehensive workmanship guarantee.

Will you remove my old roof completely?

Yes, we always recommend and perform complete tear-off of existing roofing materials. While some contractors may offer to install new shingles over existing ones to save money, this practice can hide underlying problems, add excessive weight to your roof structure, and void manufacturer warranties. Complete tear-off allows us to inspect the decking, replace any damaged wood, and ensure proper installation from the ground up.

Can I stay in my home during roof replacement?

Absolutely. While roof replacement is noisy and involves some temporary inconvenience, most homeowners remain in their homes throughout the process. We take extensive precautions to protect your property, keep work areas safe, and maintain clean conditions. If you have small children, elderly family members, or pets who may be bothered by the noise, you might consider spending the installation day away from home, but it's not required.
